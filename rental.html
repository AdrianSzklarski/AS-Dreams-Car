Lending conditions

  1. The minimum rental period is 24 hours.
  2. The Renter must have two identity documents (including a driving licence) valid on the territory of Poland for at least 12 months, the required age of the Renter is 21 years or older
  3. The vehicle may only be driven by the person declared in the contract and may not be lent to third parties.
  4. A rental day lasts 24 hours from the beginning of the rental. Extending the hire by 60 minutes beyond a full day results in charging for the next day.
  5. Rental fees are charged in advance, payment of a deposit is required - deposit amount according to the price list. Rental payments can be made in cash, by bank transfer or via a payment terminal (excluding deposit)
  6. The security deposit for the rental agreement is payable at the Lessor's premises or by transfer to the Lessor's account. It is not possible to pay the deposit via a payment terminal.
  7. The deposit paid by bank transfer shall be returned to the Lessee exclusively by bank transfer to the bank account indicated by the Lessee within 7 working days from the day of returning the vehicle.
  8. The deposit is fully refundable at the moment of returning an undamaged vehicle. The Lessor is entitled to deduct from the deposit amount any amounts related to damages, covering the costs of repairs and contractual penalties.
  9. In the case of the necessity of deducting the fees resulting from contractual penalties or the costs of car repairs from the deposit, the deposit remains at the disposal of the Lessor until the day of completing the repairs in the vehicle and from that day the time limit of 7 working days runs.
  10. Immediately after the return or replacement of the vehicle used by the Lessee by the Lessor or after the receipt of the insurer's decision on refusal to pay the compensation, the Lessor reserves the right to charge the Lessee with the following costs.
  11. OC and AC insurance is included in the rental price. AC insurance does not apply to: damage to windows, tyres, wheels, upholstery, vandalism and intentional damage to the vehicle, i.e. the vehicle is not insured;
  12. The car is handed over to the Lessee with a full fuel tank and should be returned to the Lessor with the same content, in case of returning with an incomplete tank the Lessee will be charged with costs of the missing fuel
  13. During the rental period the Lessee is obligated to carry out, at his/her own expense and effort, regular checks and replenish the oil level in the engine (1l. oil reserve is in the car), cooling liquid, tyre pressure, operation of signalling, passing and driving lights
  14. In case the vehicle is returned before the end of the contract, the amount collected for rental shall not be refunded.
  15. The user undertakes to return the vehicle undamaged, with complete equipment and documents at the place where he collected the vehicle or at another place agreed at the latest on the day of termination of the agreement.
  16. The hirer is responsible for any offences that occur during the rental period and that are related to the use of the rented vehicle, as the hirer was the owner of the vehicle. In the event of a road accident, the Renter is obliged to call the police to the site of the accident, notify the rental company and secure the car, as well as to hand over the police report and any information concerning the incident.
  17. In a situation which makes it impossible to continue driving, the Rentee is obligated to tow the vehicle to the seat of the Renter.
  18. Return to a place other than the place where the vehicle was collected is not possible. In case of such a situation, the Renter will be charged with all the costs incurred on this account.
  19. During the rental period, the Renter is obliged to pay all parking fees related to the parking place and penalties and fines imposed on the car during the rental period. In the event of non-payment of any of the penalties or fines, the Renter's personal data will be transferred to the relevant services in order to settle them
  20. The Renter does not have the right to make any repairs in the car except for changing light bulbs or repairing a wheel
  21. The Renter is obliged to keep the car clean. In the event of returning a dirty car, the following amount will be charged: 50,00 PLN - Smart; 60,00 PLN - Toyota Aygo, Toyota Yaris, Ford Fiesta and Opel Astra; 80,00 PLN - Mercedes Sprinter. These rates do not include possible seat washing (details in the Price List). This cost will be determined individually. In case of heavy dirt, the price may be increased. The car may not be used to take part in races and public events.
  22. The Renter is obliged to render all assistance to the Lessor or his insurance company in all claims or court cases in connection with the damage.
  23. A double daily rate will be charged for arbitrary extension of the contract The daily limit is 150 km (vans 200 km), for each additional kilometre a fee of PLN 0.40 will be charged.
  24. If the Lessee does not return the vehicle until 12 hours after the time indicated in the rental contract and does not contact the rental company, the rental company will inform the law enforcement agencies about the theft.
  25. In case of causing a road collision, the Renter will be charged with the cost of vehicle downtime due to repair time according to the rental price list for each day of downtime.
  26. The Renter agrees to the processing of his personal data for the purpose of this agreement in accordance with the principles of RODO in force from 25.05.2018r.
  27. Any matters not regulated by this agreement may be dealt with under the provisions of the Civil Code. Any disputes will be settled by the competent courts.
  28. The prices quoted (also on the website) include 23% VAT.
